Who we are

The Association of Computer Application (ACA) at the College of Engineering Guindy (CEG) is formulated to conduct computer applications related activities and symposiums throughout the year. With CEG's renowned legacy of excellence in technical education and its commitment to nurturing bright minds, ACA plays a crucial role in enhancing the academic experience of students through active participation. This collaboration continues CEG's tradition of guiding students towards excellence and contributing to their growth as future leaders in the field of technology.

What we do

NIRAL

Niral is a national level technical symposium organized by the postgraduate students of MCA, started from the year 2011. This is a symposium that has an implied academic aspect that distinguishes it from other symposiums. Through this symposium, every student is given an opportunity of greater degree knowledge. It would be a great platform to showcase the aspiring technical skill, co-curricular activities and come out with flying colors.
